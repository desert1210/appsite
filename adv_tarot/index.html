AI Tarot Master

Introduction

AI Tarot Master is a multilingual tarot reading application. Available languages include English, 中文, 日本語, 한국어, Español, and Français.

Features

  • Divination: Use five tarot cards to get insights into your questions.
  • Journal: A daily tarot reading using three cards to understand the day's energy.
  • Rating: Compare multiple options by drawing a card for each, revealing advantages and potential outcomes.
  • Decision: Get a simple "yes" or "no" answer to a question.

Card Selection Modes

  • Random Draw: Quick divination with random card selection.
  • Numbered Cards: Select cards by number for remote readings.
  • Categorized Selection: Use physical cards and record results in the app.
